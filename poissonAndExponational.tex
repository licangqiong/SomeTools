\section{Poisson distribution and Exponential distribution}
Poisson distribution: 

\begin{equation}
P(X=k)=\frac{e^{-\lambda}\lambda^k}{k!}
\end{equation}

Exponential distribution:
\begin{equation}
f(x;\lambda)={\lambda}e^{-\lambda x}
\end{equation}
